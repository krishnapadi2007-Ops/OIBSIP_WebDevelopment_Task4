Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist, teacher, and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India's missile and nuclear programs and inspired millions of students through his vision and dedication.

Achievements

  • Bharat Ratna Award Recipient
  • 11th President of India
  • Known as the Missile Man of India
  • Author of Wings of Fire
  • Inspired millions of young minds
